About Me

As an ICT Teacher at Creative Academy in Kritipur, I bring together my passion for education and technology to create engaging, innovative learning experiences. With a background in Education and Information Technology, I specialize in integrating digital tools into educational practices to enhance student engagement and outcomes.

Currently pursuing a B.Tech ED at Kathmandu University School of Education, I have a strong foundation in educational principles, instructional design models and educational psychology. My approach to teaching goes beyond traditional lecture-based methods, incorporating diverse instructional techniques to create student-centered learning environments.

My teaching philosophy centers on leveraging technology to create inclusive, accessible education while developing crucial digital literacy skills that prepare students for their future careers. I believe in continuous innovation and staying current with emerging educational technologies to provide the best possible learning experiences.
